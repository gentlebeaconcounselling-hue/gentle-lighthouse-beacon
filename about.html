My Approach to Counselling

Driven by a passion for helping others, I have trained in various modalities for managing anxiety, post-trauma and self-esteem issues, and learning self-compassion. I specialize in healing before forgiveness and work with my clients to develop effective strategies for coping with their personal challenges.

Whether you're navigating anxiety, burnout, or life transitions, this is a space where you can anchor and reflect. I believe each of us carries an inner compass, even when the path feels lost.

In my practice, I serve as a beacon—helping you reconnect with your sense of direction, resilience, and authentic self. Together, we safely explore, gain clarity, and move forward with greater confidence.
