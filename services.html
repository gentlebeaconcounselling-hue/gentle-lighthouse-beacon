Services I Offer

I offer gentle, compassionate support tailored to your needs. Each session is a space to reflect, heal, and grow.

Individual Counselling (Adults & Older Teens)

One-on-one support for adults and older teens navigating emotional challenges, life changes, or personal growth.

Healing Before Forgiveness Therapy

A trauma-informed approach that honors your healing journey before exploring forgiveness—at your own pace.

Grief, Chronic Pain & Trauma Support

Gentle guidance through grief, long-term pain, and trauma—creating space for comfort, resilience, and healing.

Mindfulness & Stress Management

Practical tools and calming practices to help you manage stress, anxiety, and emotional overwhelm.

Life Transitions Support

Support through career shifts, relationship changes, relocation, or other major life transitions.

Anxiety Support

Gentle strategies to manage worry, overwhelm, and panic—helping you feel more grounded and present.

Trauma Recovery

A safe, non-judgmental space to process past experiences and begin healing at your own pace.

Self-Esteem & Identity

Support in building confidence, self-worth, and reconnecting with your authentic self.
